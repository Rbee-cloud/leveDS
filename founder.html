The Vision Behind Leve

Meet the passionate founder whose journey led to creating a brand that empowers wellness and sustainability

Isabella Santos

With a background in environmental science and a passion for holistic wellness, Isabella founded Leve in 2018 after witnessing the disconnect between modern lifestyles and sustainable practices.

Her journey began during a transformative trip to the Amazon rainforest, where she learned about traditional plant-based remedies from indigenous communities. This experience sparked her mission to bridge ancient wisdom with modern wellness needs.

'Wellness shouldn't be complicated or come at the expense of our planet. At Leve, we believe in creating products that are good for you and gentle on the Earth.'

Under Isabella's leadership, Leve has grown from a small kitchen experiment to a recognized brand in the wellness industry, all while maintaining its commitment to sustainability and ethical practices.
